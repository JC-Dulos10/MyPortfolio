SHAKES

a sweet, cold beverage that is usually made from milk, ice cream or ice milk, and flavorings or sweeteners such as butterscotch, caramel sauce, chocolate syrup, or fruit syrup. Many more precise and rigid definitions are used, depending on the location.

  • Mango shake
  • Buko shake
  • Milkshake


Mango shake is a fruit shake that is made from mango which is usually made from mango, milk and crushed ice.




Buko shake is a fruit shake that is made from buko which is usually made from buko, milk and crushed ice.




Milkshake is a sweet, cold beverage that is usually made from milk. ice cream or ice milk and flavorings or sweetners like caramel sauce.
