Coffee shop





COFFEE

Black coffee is normal coffee without additives like sugar and cream/milk after grinding and brewing the coffee beans.
A latte is a coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk.
Caramel Macchiato is espresso-based coffee made with vanilla syrup, steamed milk, espresso and caramel sauce.




TEA

Jasmine Tea is tea scented with the aroma of Jasmine blossoms.
Green Tea is a type of tea that is made from Camellia sinesis leaves and buds that have not undergone the same withering and oxidation process used to make oolong teas and black teas.
Mint Tea is a herbal tea made by infusing mint leaves in hot water, it usually made from spearmint or peppermint leaves.




SHAKES

Mango shake is a fruit shake that is made from mango which is usually made from mango, milk and crushed ice.
Buko shake is a fruit shake that is made from buko which is usually made from buko, milk and crushed ice.
Milkshake is a sweet, cold beverage that is usually made from milk. ice cream or ice milk and flavorings or sweetners like caramel sauce.
